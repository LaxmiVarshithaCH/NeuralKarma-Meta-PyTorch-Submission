How NeuralKarma Works

1. Real Data

Trained on 165K+ real utterances from Allen AI's ProsocialDialog and Hendrycks' ETHICS benchmark — downloaded from HuggingFace.

2. ML Scoring

6 trained classifiers (TF-IDF + Logistic Regression) score each input across Prosociality, Harm Avoidance, Fairness, Virtue, and Duty.

3. Karma Physics

Scores undergo temporal decay (e−λt, 7-day half-life) and ripple propagation through social networks.
